Ask less. Know more

Quick answers to the things you’re wondering—so you can get back to living smarter.

What is LyteRide and how does it work?

LyteRide is an electric bicycle built for delivery riders. It uses a rechargeable battery to give you extra power, so you can ride faster and with less effort.

How far can I ride on a full battery?

On a full charge, you can ride up to 60 km, depending on road conditions and how much weight you’re carrying.

What happens if my battery runs low during deliveries?

No stress — just visit a LyteRide Swap Station, switch your empty battery for a full one, and keep going without delay.

Can I track my bike if it gets lost or stolen?

Yes. Every LyteRide comes with built-in tracking, so you can locate it if it ever goes missing.

Is LyteRide cheaper to run than using fuel bikes?

Yes. Charging or swapping batteries costs far less than buying fuel, and there’s less maintenance too.

Where can I swap my battery in Lagos?

We’re setting up Swap Stations in key areas where delivery riders work most, so you’re never far from a battery replacement.

How strong and durable is the bike for Nigerian roads?

LyteRide is built tough with strong frames and quality tires made to handle Lagos roads and daily delivery use.
